Adrienne Leigh Krause

Contemporary Dancer | Philadelphia

Bio

Adrienne Krause is a Philadelphia based contemporary dancer. She has performed in various showcases in the Northeast United States including Maine, New York City, and Philadelphia. Krause's movement is emotionally evocative.

Interests

Adrienne has an interest in plants, a hobby for crocheting, and a deep love for her cats.
